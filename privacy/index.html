Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026
Data We Collect How We Use It Data Sharing Account Deletion Contact

GaiaTakas is a mobile-first marketplace for swaps, gifts, and local item circulation. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how users can request support or deletion.

Data We Collect

  • Basic account data such as display name, email address, or verified phone number.
  • Profile content such as avatar, bio, handle, and city or location details.
  • Listing content such as titles, descriptions, categories, photos, and swap or gift metadata.
  • Message and conversation data needed to operate offers, swap flows, and support moderation.
  • Technical data such as push notification tokens, session records, and basic request logs.

How We Use It

  • To create and secure user accounts.
  • To show nearby listings and make distance filters work correctly.
  • To operate messaging, reporting, moderation, and swap lifecycle actions.
  • To diagnose reliability, abuse, fraud, and safety issues.

Data Sharing

GaiaTakas does not sell user data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers that help us run the service, such as hosting, messaging, or payment partners. We do not share user data with third-party advertising networks for ad targeting.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly inside the app. When an account is deleted, active session access is revoked and account data is removed according to the current service logic and platform constraints.

Contact

For privacy or safety questions, contact hello@ztudio.co.
