Community Guidelines

GaiaTakas is built for useful circulation, not clutter, spam, or risky behavior. These rules help keep the marketplace credible for real users.

What belongs here

  • Real items you own and can hand off.
  • Honest photos and descriptions.
  • Respectful negotiation and clear communication.
  • Gift listings that are actually free.

What is not allowed

  • Counterfeit, stolen, dangerous, or illegal items.
  • Adult sexual content, hate content, threats, or harassment.
  • Spam messages, scam behavior, or payment manipulation.
  • Impersonation or misleading account identity.

Meeting safely

  • Prefer public locations for handoff.
  • Do not rush into off-platform payment requests.
  • If a conversation feels unsafe, stop and report it.

Reporting and moderation

Users can report listings or users directly inside GaiaTakas. Reports may lead to listing removal, messaging restrictions, or account suspension after review.
